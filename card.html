Grey Wolf

Wolves have higher success hunting alone or in pairs than they do in packs.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Average Height: 80 to 50cm
  • Average Lifespan: 5 to 6 Years
  • Habitat: Wilderness

Grey wolves are native to the wildernesses of Eurasia and North America. It is considered the largest member of its family. Grey wolves tend to live in packs of 5 to 11 members but can be as large as 42 members. They are highly territorial animals and go to great lengths to protect thier territory.
